About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Center for Mathematics and Artificial Intelligence at George Mason University, working with Dr. Harbir Antil. Previously I was an NSERC Postdoctoral Fellow and Lecturer in the Department of Computational Applied Mathematics and Operations Research (CMOR) at Rice University working with Dr. Beatrice Riviere. My research interests lie broadly in the numerical and mathematical analysis of partial differential equations, nonsmooth optimization, and PDE constrained optimization. I hold Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from the University of Waterloo under the supervision of Dr. Sander Rhebergen. My graduate studies were supported by NSERC through the Alexander Graham Bell Canadian Graduate Scholarship program, and I was awarded the University of Waterloo’s 2022/2023 Applied Mathematics Department’s Doctoral Prize.
